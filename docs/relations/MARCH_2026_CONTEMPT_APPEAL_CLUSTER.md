## Purpose

This relation note captures the procedural chain that links the **09 February 2026 contempt application**, the **26 March 2026 dismissal with punitive costs**, and the **30 March 2026 leave-to-appeal notice**. The direct judgment shows that this cluster is not merely a routine appellate step. It is a **weaponised process sequence** in which disputed stock and training incidents were pressed into a contempt framework and then immediately escalated after failure.
