## 2. KEY AREAS OF TAX FRAUD

### 2.1. Fabrication of Financial Statements (RegimaSA)

- On **25 June 2025**, financial statements for RegimaSA for the 2019 fiscal year were fabricated and signed by Peter Faucitt. This was **more than 6 years after the period end**.
- The company had **zero revenue** in 2019. The creation of these retrospective, misleading accounts was an act of **fabricating evidence** to support the broader fraud and likely to mislead SARS.
- **MAT4719 Annexure PF2** (pages 45–51) shows RegimA Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd was formerly "Clear Creek Trading 139" — the company name change history is relevant to the fabrication timeline.

### 2.2. R5.4M Stock Write-Off & VAT Fraud (Strategic Logistics)

- A fraudulent stock write-off of **R5,400,000** was processed for Strategic Logistics. This artificially reduced the company's profit and, consequently, its income tax liability.
- This write-off also constitutes **VAT fraud**, as it would have been used to claim an unlawful VAT refund or reduce VAT payable.
- **MAT4719 Annexure PF4** (pages 56–58) provides the WinDeed report for Strategic Logistics CC (2008/136496/23), confirming the corporate structure.

### 2.3. Revenue Diversion & Income Tax Evasion

- Over **R3.14M** in revenue was diverted from the legitimate company accounts to accounts controlled by the perpetrators.
- This income was never declared, resulting in **income tax evasion**.

### 2.4. Mischaracterisation of Legitimate Business Expenses

- **MAT4719 Annexure PF9** (pages 102–137) documents **R6,738,007** in credit card transactions across two accounts (4910505191 and 4910505225) from January 2024 to February 2025.
- **MAT4719 Annexure PF10** (pages 138–161) documents **R2,116,159.47** in categorised expenses from March to July 2025, broken down by: Computer Platforms (3300/020), Software (3300/030), Shopify Connectors (3300/040), Apps (3300/050), and Other (3300/060).
- These are payments to standard technology vendors (Shopify, GitHub, Cloudflare, Google, Microsoft, etc.) for legitimate e-commerce operations. Peter's characterisation of these as "misappropriation" in his founding affidavit may have tax implications if used to claim losses or deductions.

### 2.5. Complicity of the Oversight Chain

- The entire financial oversight chain was compromised:
    - **Rynette Farrar** (Bookkeeper) had an unusually close relationship with a SARS official, Anton Hechter, suggesting insider manipulation.
    - **Daniel Bantjies** (External Accountant) had a conflict of interest as a trustee and failed to act on fraud reports. His confirmatory affidavit (MAT4719 pages 69–71) was sworn 68 days after receiving Daniel Faucitt's fraud report.
    - **Peter Faucitt** (Director) was the ultimate beneficiary.
