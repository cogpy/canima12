## 1. INTRODUCTION

This report details evidence of systematic tax fraud within the RegimA group of companies, orchestrated by Mr. Peter Andrew Faucitt, Ms. Rynette Farrar, and Mr. D.J. Bantjies. The evidence points to the fabrication of financial statements, the concealment of income, and the fraudulent manipulation of expenses to evade tax liabilities.

## 2. SUMMARY OF ALLEGATIONS

| Allegation | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Fabrication of Financial Statements** | Creation of fraudulent 2019 financial statements for a company that only existed from March 2021. |
| **Concealment of Income** | Diversion of revenue from auditable systems (Shopify) to an opaque, controlled system (Sage Pastel). |
| **Fraudulent Expense Manipulation** | Dumping of two years of unallocated expenses into one company (RegimA Worldwide) to artificially reduce profits. |
| **VAT Fraud** | Suspected diversion of sales from VAT-registered entities to non-registered entities to evade VAT payments. |

## 3. DETAILED ALLEGATIONS & EVIDENCE

### 3.1. Fabrication of Financial Statements

*   **The Evidence:** Emails between Rynette Farrar and an accountant from De Novo prove that Ms. Farrar instructed the creation of fraudulent 2019 financial statements for a company name that was only registered in March 2021.
*   **The Forgery:** Mr. Peter Faucitt signed these fabricated accounts as director for a year in which he was not appointed.
*   **The Significance:** This is irrefutable proof of a deliberate act to create a false financial history for the company, likely to secure financing or mislead SARS.

### 3.2. Concealment of Income

*   **The Mechanism:** The syndicate systematically dismantled the transparent and auditable Shopify e-commerce platform and diverted all sales to an opaque Sage Pastel system controlled exclusively by Ms. Rynette Farrar.
*   **The Intent:** This move was designed to conceal the true revenue of the company, making it impossible for SARS and other stakeholders to verify income.

### 3.3. Fraudulent Expense Manipulation

*   **The Scheme:** In March 2025, Rynette Farrar and Peter Faucitt dumped two years of unallocated expenses from all companies in the group into a single entity, RegimA Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd.
*   **The Coercion:** They then pressured the director of RegimA Worldwide, Mr. Daniel James Faucitt, to sign off on these fraudulent accounts for SARS VAT and Annual Accounts submissions within 12 hours.

## 4. REQUEST FOR INVESTIGATION

We urge the NPA to launch a full investigation into these serious allegations of tax fraud. The evidence points to a long-term, coordinated scheme to defraud the South African Revenue Service (SARS) of millions of rands in tax revenue. We are prepared to provide all documentary evidence in our possession to assist with the investigation.
