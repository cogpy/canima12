## 2. Independent Evidence Anchors

1. **Bank-Account-Emptying Pattern (RegimA SA).** FNB statements for the operating account of RegimA SA show a balance movement from above R2,500,000 to R5,284 between October 2024 and March 2025, with no corresponding payments to legitimate suppliers identifiable in the same period.

2. **Post-Interdict Extraction Window (RWD).** R2,850,000 was extracted from RWD's operating account between 26 August and 11 September 2025, the immediate post-grant window of the *ex parte* order of Khumalo J dated 19 August 2025. The extraction is inconsistent with the order's stated purpose of preserving the entity's funds.

3. **Customer Redirection to Related-Party Domain.** More than 100 customer-facing communications during March — September 2025 instructed payment to bank accounts associated with Adderory (Pty) Ltd via the `regimaskin.co.za` domain, of which Darren Dennis Farrar (son of Subject Rynette Farrar) is a director.

4. **Sage Subscription Expiry (Records Concealment).** The deliberate expiry of the Sage accounting subscription on 23 July 2025 obstructs any reporting institution's ability to discharge its s.21 customer-due-diligence obligations.
