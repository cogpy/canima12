# Replying Affidavit Skeleton

    **Rule / source:** Rule 6(5)(e)  
**Use:** To reply to new matter raised in an answering affidavit.  
    **When not to use:** Do not reconstruct the founding case or introduce impermissible new matter without leave.

    ## Template

    ```text
    REPLYING AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[FULL NAME]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the Applicant / authorised representative of the Applicant.
2. I deliver this replying affidavit in response to the answering affidavit of [DEPONENT] dated [DATE].
3. I reply only to material new matter raised in answer.

4. Ad paragraph [x] of the answering affidavit:
   4.1 [response].

5. To the extent necessary, the following allegations are specifically denied:
   [detail].

6. Save as set out above, I persist in the relief sought in the Notice of Motion.

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
    ```
