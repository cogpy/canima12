    # General Notice of Motion Template

    **Category:** Motion Proceedings / Rule 6  
    **Purpose:** Launch an application proceeding by notice of motion with supporting affidavit material.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use for action proceedings commenced by summons.

    ## Template

    ```text
    IN THE HIGH COURT OF SOUTH AFRICA
([DIVISION])

CASE NO: [YYYY-NNNNNN]

In the matter between:
[PARTY NAMES AS CITED]

NOTICE OF MOTION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant hereby applies to the above Honourable Court on a date to be arranged with the Registrar for an order in the following terms:

1. [Primary relief].
2. [Ancillary relief].
3. Costs of this application [on the scale of / only in the event of opposition].
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the accompanying founding affidavit of [Deponent] will be used in support of this application.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that any party intending to oppose this application must deliver a notice of intention to oppose within the time prescribed by the Rules and applicable directives, and thereafter deliver answering affidavit(s) within the time so prescribed.

DATED at [Place] on this [Day] day of [Month] [Year].

______________________________
[Attorney / Applicant in person]
Address for service: [Address]
Email: [Email]
Tel: [Number]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL RESPONDENTS / THEIR ATTORNEYS
    ```
