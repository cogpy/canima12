    # Replying Affidavit Skeleton Template

    **Category:** Motion Proceedings / Rule 6  
    **Purpose:** Reply to material new matter raised in an answering affidavit.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use it to reconstruct or enlarge the founding case impermissibly.

    ## Template

    ```text
    REPLYING AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[Full Name]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the Applicant / authorised representative of the Applicant.
2. I file this replying affidavit in response to the answering affidavit of [Name] dated [Date].
3. I reply only to material new matter raised in answer.

4. Ad paragraph [x] of the answering affidavit:
   4.1 [response].

5. Save as appears herein, I persist in the contents of the founding affidavit and the relief set out in the Notice of Motion.

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
    ```
