    # Answering Affidavit Skeleton Template

    **Category:** Motion Proceedings / Rule 6  
    **Purpose:** Provide the substantive response of a respondent opposing motion relief.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use in place of a plea in action proceedings.

    ## Template

    ```text
    ANSWERING AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[Full Name]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the [First / Second / nth] Respondent in this matter.
2. I oppose the relief sought by the Applicant.
3. Save where expressly admitted, every allegation inconsistent herewith is denied.

PRELIMINARY ISSUES
4. At the outset I raise the following preliminary issues:
   4.1 [urgency denied / non-joinder / authority / jurisdiction / etc.]

RESPONSE TO FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS
5. Ad paragraph [x] thereof:
   5.1 I admit / deny / do not admit the contents.
   5.2 The true position is [detail].

AFFIRMATIVE VERSION
6. The correct chronology and factual position are as follows:
   6.1 [detail].
   6.2 [detail].

LEGAL RESPONSE
7. The application falls to be dismissed because [grounds].

RELIEF
8. I accordingly pray that the application be dismissed with costs, alternatively with such order as the Court deems fit.

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
    ```
