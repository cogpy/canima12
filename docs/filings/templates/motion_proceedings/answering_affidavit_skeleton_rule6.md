# Answering Affidavit Skeleton

    **Rule / source:** Rule 6(5)(d)  
**Use:** To answer a motion application on the merits and any preliminary issues.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use it in place of a plea in action proceedings.

    ## Template

    ```text
    ANSWERING AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[FULL NAME]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the [First / Second / n-th] Respondent in this matter.
2. I oppose the relief sought by the Applicant.
3. Save where expressly admitted, every allegation in the founding papers inconsistent herewith is denied.

PART A: PRELIMINARY ISSUES
4. At the outset I raise the following preliminary issues:
   4.1 [urgency denied / lack of jurisdiction / non-joinder / authority / etc.]

PART B: RESPONSE TO FACTUAL ALLEGATIONS
5. Ad paragraph [x] thereof:
   5.1 I admit / deny / do not admit the contents.
   5.2 The true position is [detail].

PART C: AFFIRMATIVE VERSION
6. The correct chronology is as follows: [detail].

PART D: LEGAL RESPONSE
7. The application falls to be dismissed because [grounds].

PART E: RELIEF
8. I accordingly pray that the application be dismissed with costs, alternatively with such order as the Court deems fit.

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
    ```
