    # Founding Affidavit Skeleton Template

    **Category:** Motion Proceedings / Rule 6  
    **Purpose:** Provide the primary evidentiary foundation for motion relief.  
    **When not to use:** Do not treat a bare notice of motion as a substitute for a proper founding affidavit.

    ## Template

    ```text
    FOUNDING AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[Full Name]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am an adult [occupation / capacity] residing at [address].
2. I am the [Applicant / duly authorised representative of the Applicant].
3. The facts contained herein are, unless the context indicates otherwise, within my personal knowledge and are true and correct.
4. This affidavit is filed in support of the accompanying Notice of Motion.

PARTIES
5. The Applicant is [description].
6. The First Respondent is [description].
7. The Second Respondent is [description].

JURISDICTION
8. This Court has jurisdiction because [basis].

FACTUAL CHRONOLOGY
9. On [date], [event].
10. On [date], [event].
11. The material chronology is as follows: [detail].

LEGAL BASIS
12. The relief sought is competent under [Rule / statute / common law].
13. The requirements for that relief are met because [grounds].

URGENCY [IF APPLICABLE]
14. This matter is urgent because [grounds].
15. The Applicant cannot obtain substantial redress in due course because [grounds].

PREJUDICE / BALANCE / INTERESTS
16. The prejudice to the Applicant is [detail].
17. The prejudice to the Respondent is [detail], which is outweighed / not irreparable because [detail].

RELIEF
18. I accordingly pray for the relief set out in the Notice of Motion.

DEPONENT: __________________

THUS SIGNED AND SWORN TO before me at [Place] on [Date], the Deponent having acknowledged that [he / she / they] knows and understands the contents of this affidavit, has no objection to taking the prescribed oath, and considers the oath binding on [his / her / their] conscience.

COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
    ```
