# General Notice of Motion

    **Rule / source:** Rule 6  
**Use:** To launch an application proceeding supported by affidavit evidence.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use for action proceedings commenced by summons.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant hereby applies to the above Honourable Court on a date to be arranged with the Registrar for an order in the following terms:

1. [Primary relief].
2. [Ancillary relief].
3. Costs of this application [on the scale of / only in the event of opposition].
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the accompanying founding affidavit of [DEPONENT] will be used in support of this application.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the Respondent(s) who intend to oppose this application are required:
(a) to deliver notice of intention to oppose within the prescribed period; and
(b) thereafter to deliver answering affidavit(s) within the period prescribed by the Rules, the notice, or the applicable directive.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEY / APPLICANT IN PERSON]
Address for service: [ADDRESS]
Email: [EMAIL]
Tel: [NUMBER]
Ref: [REFERENCE]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE RESPONDENT(S) / THEIR ATTORNEYS
    ```
