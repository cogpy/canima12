# Notice of Intention to Oppose

    **Rule / source:** Rule 6(5)(d)(i)  
**Use:** To enter opposition in motion proceedings after service of a notice of motion or application.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use this as the response to a summons in action proceedings.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF INTENTION TO OPPOSE

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Respondent / n-th Respondent] hereby gives notice of [his/her/its] intention to oppose the application launched by the Applicant.

The Respondent appoints the address set out below as the address at which [he/she/it] will accept service of all further process, notices and documents in this matter.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / PARTY IN PERSON]
Address for service: [ADDRESS]
Email: [EMAIL]
Tel: [NUMBER]
Ref: [REFERENCE]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE APPLICANT'S ATTORNEYS
    ```
