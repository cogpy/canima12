# Replying Affidavit Skeleton — Rule 6(5)(e)
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

**Use:** Applicant's reply confined to new matter in answer.  
**When not to use:** Do not use to reconstruct the founding case.

## Template

```text
REPLYING AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[FULL NAME]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the Applicant / authorised representative of the Applicant.
2. I deliver this replying affidavit in response to the answering affidavit
   of [DEPONENT] dated [DATE].
3. I reply only to material new matter raised in answer.

4. Ad paragraph [x] of the answering affidavit:
   4.1 [response].

5. To the extent necessary, the following allegations are specifically denied:
   [detail].

6. Save as set out above, I persist in the relief sought in the Notice of Motion.

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
```
