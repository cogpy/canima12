    # Notice Opposing Leave to Appeal Template

    **Category:** Appeals and Review / Rule 49 context  
    **Purpose:** Record respondent-side opposition to an application for leave to appeal.  
    **When not to use:** Do not mistake this for the fuller heads of argument or affidavit process where further amplification is allowed or required.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE / SUBMISSION OPPOSING APPLICATION FOR LEAVE TO APPEAL

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Respondent / Plaintiff / Defendant] opposes the application for leave to appeal dated [date].

The grounds of opposition include the following:
1. The proposed appeal enjoys no reasonable prospects of success.
2. No compelling reason exists for the appeal to be heard.
3. The grounds advanced do not displace the factual and legal findings in the judgment dated [date].
4. Costs should follow the result.

The opposing party reserves the right to amplify these grounds in written submissions, affidavit material where permitted, and/or heads of argument.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys / Party in person]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE APPELLANT / APPLICANT / ATTORNEYS
    ```
