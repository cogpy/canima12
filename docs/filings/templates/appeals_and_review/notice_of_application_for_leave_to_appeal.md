# Notice of Application for Leave to Appeal — Rule 49 Context
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

**Use:** Civil appeal step after judgment.  
**When not to use:** Do not use as a substitute for a full appeal record or heads of argument.

## Template

```text
NOTICE OF APPLICATION FOR LEAVE TO APPEAL

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Applicant / Plaintiff / Respondent / Defendant]
hereby applies for leave to appeal against the whole / part of the judgment
and order of [Judge] handed down on [date], to the Full Court of the
[Division], alternatively the Supreme Court of Appeal.

The grounds of appeal are the following:
1. [Ground 1].
2. [Ground 2].
3. [Ground 3].

The relief sought on appeal will be:
(a) that the appeal be upheld;
(b) that the order appealed against be set aside / varied; and
(c) that such further order be granted as the appeal court deems fit.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / PARTY IN PERSON]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
```
