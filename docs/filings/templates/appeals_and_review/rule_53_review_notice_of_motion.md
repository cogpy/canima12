# Rule 53 Review Notice of Motion
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

## Template

```text
NOTICE OF MOTION: REVIEW IN TERMS OF RULE 53

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:

1. Reviewing and setting aside the decision of [functionary / tribunal / officer]
   taken on [date].
2. Directing the Respondent decision-maker to dispatch, within 15 days after
   receipt hereof, the record of the proceedings sought to be reviewed,
   together with reasons if not already furnished.
3. Granting the Applicant leave to supplement this application after receipt
   of the record.
4. Costs.
5. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE DECISION-MAKER / RESPONDENTS
```
