# Notice Opposing Leave to Appeal / Respondent-Side Appellate Response — Rule 49 Context

## Template

```text
NOTICE / SUBMISSION OPPOSING APPLICATION FOR LEAVE TO APPEAL

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Respondent / Plaintiff / Defendant] opposes
the application for leave to appeal dated [date].

The grounds of opposition include the following:
1. The proposed appeal enjoys no reasonable prospects of success.
2. No compelling reason exists for the appeal to be heard.
3. The grounds advanced do not displace the factual and legal findings in
   the judgment dated [date].
4. Costs should follow the result.

The opposing party reserves the right to amplify these grounds in written
submissions, affidavit material where permitted, and/or heads of argument.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / PARTY IN PERSON]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE APPELLANT / APPLICANT / ATTORNEYS
```
