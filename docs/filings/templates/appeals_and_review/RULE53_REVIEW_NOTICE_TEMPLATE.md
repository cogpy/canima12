    # Rule 53 Review Notice Template

    **Category:** Appeals and Review / Rule 53  
    **Purpose:** Launch review proceedings and call for dispatch of the record.  
    **When not to use:** Do not omit the record-dispatch relief where the Rule 53 machinery is being invoked.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: REVIEW IN TERMS OF RULE 53

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:

1. Reviewing and setting aside the decision of [functionary / tribunal / officer] taken on [date].
2. Directing the Respondent decision-maker to dispatch, within 15 days after receipt hereof, the record of the proceedings sought to be reviewed, together with reasons if not already furnished.
3. Granting the Applicant leave to supplement this application after receipt of the record.
4. Costs.
5. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [Deponent] will be used in support.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE DECISION-MAKER / RESPONDENTS
    ```
