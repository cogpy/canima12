# Notice of Application for Leave to Appeal

    **Rule / source:** Rule 49 context / section 17  
**Use:** To seek leave to appeal against a judgment or order.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use as a substitute for appeal heads, records, or later appellate steps.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF APPLICATION FOR LEAVE TO APPEAL

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Applicant / Plaintiff / Respondent / Defendant] hereby applies for leave to appeal against the whole / part of the judgment and order of [Judge] handed down on [date], to the Full Court of the [Division], alternatively the Supreme Court of Appeal.

The grounds of appeal are the following:
1. [Ground 1].
2. [Ground 2].
3. [Ground 3].

The relief sought on appeal will be:
(a) that the appeal be upheld;
(b) that the order appealed against be set aside / varied; and
(c) that such further order be granted as the appeal court deems fit.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / PARTY IN PERSON]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
    ```
