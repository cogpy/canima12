## Immediate-Use Matrix

This matrix converts the eight supplied templates into a posture-aware checklist for immediate use in Case 2025-137857. It is designed to prevent the two most common procedural failures in complex electronic litigation: **using the wrong document type**, and **using the right document at the wrong stage**.[1] [2]

| Document type | Present usefulness | Trigger condition in this case | Minimum prerequisites | Red-team misuse warning |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Amicus curiae application | Low / exceptional | A non-party body with real legal-assistance value seeks admission | Defined issue of law, proposed submissions, non-duplication explanation | Do not use where the non-party actually has a direct and substantial interest requiring joinder |
| Joinder application | Medium / conditional | A necessary party is absent from relief that may bind them | Precise identification of the absent party and their direct-and-substantial interest | Do not use as a general "widen the case" mechanism |
| Notice of appointment as attorney of record | High | Representation is newly formalized or changed | Signed mandate, attorney details, address for service | Does not itself grant CourtOnline access |
| Notice of intention to defend | Low | A separate action is served by summons | Summons served, dies induciae running | Do not use for motion proceedings |
| Notice of intention to oppose | Conditional / historical | A fresh application is served and opposition must be entered | Notice of motion served, opposition deadline running | Do not use as an answer to a summons |
| Notice to abide | Medium / conditional | A nominal or neutral party wishes to stay in the file without opposing merits | Clear position on merits and costs | Do not file if the party in truth disputes substantive relief |
| Request to access a case | High | A newly appointed attorney or properly participating party needs CourtOnline access | Proof of role, filed notice/order, identity/practitioner details | Does not itself place substantive opposition or relief before the court |
| Rule 37(2)(b) and (7) notice | Very low for main file | A separate action has reached close of pleadings and pre-trial stage | Action proceedings, pleadings closed, discovery completed or substantially complete | Do not use in the present motion-and-appeal posture |

## Recommended Sequence by Practical Need

| Practical need | First document | Second document | Third document if needed |
|---|---|---|---|
| New attorney enters the matter | Notice of appointment as attorney of record | Request to access a case | Substantive filing appropriate to the stage |
| New motion is launched against respondents | Notice of intention to oppose | Access request if needed | Answering affidavit / other Rule 6 step |
| Separate action is launched | Notice of intention to defend | Access request if on CourtOnline | Plea / exception / further action-stage step |
| Absent necessary party identified | Joinder application | Access request after order, if relevant | Subsequent affidavit or notice by joined party |
| Neutral institutional party remains in file | Notice to abide | Access request if docket visibility required | Costs-limited appearance if needed |
