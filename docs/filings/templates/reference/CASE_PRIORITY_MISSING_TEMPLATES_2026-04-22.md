## Purpose

This pack is narrower than the full Uniform Rules library. It concentrates on the templates that are most likely to matter in the present case environment: **Rule 7 authority disputes**, **Rule 30 and 30A irregular-step practice**, **Rule 42 rescission / variation architecture**, **Rule 27 condonation or removal-of-bar relief**, and the **core Rule 6 affidavit sequence** that supports interlocutory and corrective motion work.[1] [2] [3]

Each template below is still a skeleton. It is designed to reduce drafting latency while preserving posture discipline.

## 1. Rule 7 Notice Challenging Authority to Act

```text
NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 7(1)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [First / Second Respondent] hereby disputes
the authority of [NAME OF ATTORNEY / REPRESENTATIVE / FIRM], who has
purported to act on behalf of [PARTY], to so act in these proceedings.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the said [person / firm / representative] is
required, within 10 court days of service hereof, to satisfy the Court
that [he/she/it] is duly authorised to act on behalf of the said party in
this matter.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that, failing such proof of authority, the steps taken
by such person may be challenged and such further relief as may be
appropriate may be sought.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________

Ref: [REFERENCE]

TO: [ATTORNEYS / REPRESENTATIVE WHOSE AUTHORITY IS CHALLENGED]
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / THEIR ATTORNEYS
```

## 8. Core Founding Affidavit Skeleton for Interlocutory Relief

```text
FOUNDING AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[FULL NAME]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the [First / Second Respondent / authorised representative] in this matter.
2. The facts contained herein are, unless the context indicates otherwise,
   within my personal knowledge and are true and correct.
3. This affidavit is filed in support of the accompanying Notice of Motion.

4. The relief sought is [identify relief].
5. The procedural foundation for such relief is [Rule 27 / Rule 30 / Rule 30A / Rule 42 / other].
6. The relevant chronology is as follows:
   6.1 [date and event].
   6.2 [date and event].
   6.3 [date and event].
7. The procedural defect / non-compliance / basis for rescission is [detail].
8. The prejudice suffered is [detail].
9. The relief sought is just and equitable because [detail].
10. I accordingly pray for the relief set out in the Notice of Motion.

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
```

```text
3. Save where expressly admitted, the allegations inconsistent herewith are denied.

4. As a preliminary matter, I raise the following issues:
   4.1 [urgency / authority / non-joinder / irregularity / prejudice].

5. Ad paragraph [x] of the founding affidavit:
   5.1 [response].

6. The correct chronology and procedural position are as follows:
   6.1 [detail].
   6.2 [detail].

7. The application falls to be dismissed because [legal and factual grounds].
8. I accordingly pray that the application be dismissed with costs,
   alternatively with such order as the Court deems fit.

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
```
