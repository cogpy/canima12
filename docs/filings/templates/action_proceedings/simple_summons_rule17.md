# Simple Summons

    **Rule / source:** Rule 17  
**Use:** To commence action proceedings for liquidated claims or other claims suitable for simple summons.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use for motion proceedings or where full particulars of claim are required immediately.

    ## Template

    ```text
    SIMPLE SUMMONS

The Plaintiff claims from the Defendant:
1. Payment of the sum of [amount].
2. Interest thereon at [rate] from [date] to date of payment.
3. Costs of suit.
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

The cause of action is [briefly state liquidated claim].

YOU ARE HEREBY SUMMONED that within 10 court days after service of this summons upon you, you give notice to the Plaintiff of your intention to defend this action, failing which judgment may be granted against you.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS]
    ```
