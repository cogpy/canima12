    # Notice of Intention to Defend Template

    **Category:** Action Proceedings / Rule 19  
    **Purpose:** Enter appearance to defend in action proceedings.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use as the gateway notice in motion proceedings.

    ## Template

    ```text
    IN THE HIGH COURT OF SOUTH AFRICA
([DIVISION])

CASE NO: [YYYY-NNNNNN]

In the matter between:
[PARTY NAMES AS CITED]

NOTICE OF INTENTION TO DEFEND

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Defendant] hereby gives notice of [his / her / its] intention to defend the action instituted against [him / her / it] in the above matter.

The Defendant appoints the address below as the address at which all further process may be served.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys / Defendant in person]
Address for service: [Address]
Email: [Email]
Tel: [Number]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS
    ```
