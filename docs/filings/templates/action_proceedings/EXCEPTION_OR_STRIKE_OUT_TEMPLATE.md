    # Exception or Strike-Out Template

    **Category:** Action Proceedings / Rule 23  
    **Purpose:** Object to a defective pleading or seek strike-out of scandalous, vexatious, or irrelevant matter.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where the complaint is better framed as a Rule 30 or Rule 30A irregularity point.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF EXCEPTION / APPLICATION TO STRIKE OUT

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant] hereby excepts to the [particulars of claim / plea / declaration / replication] on the grounds that:

1. [It discloses no cause of action / no defence].
2. [It is vague and embarrassing].
3. [It contains scandalous, vexatious, or irrelevant matter], namely [detail].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the excipient will apply to this Honourable Court for an order upholding the exception / striking out the impugned matter, with costs, and for such further relief as may be just.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE OTHER PARTY / ATTORNEYS
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
    ```
