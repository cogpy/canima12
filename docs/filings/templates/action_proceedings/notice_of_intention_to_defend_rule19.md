# Notice of Intention to Defend

    **Rule / source:** Rule 19  
**Use:** To enter a defence in action proceedings after service of summons.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use as the gateway notice in motion proceedings.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF INTENTION TO DEFEND

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [n-th] Defendant hereby gives notice of [his/her/its] intention to defend the action instituted by the Plaintiff.

The Defendant appoints the address below as the address at which all further process may be served.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / DEFENDANT IN PERSON]
Address for service: [ADDRESS]
Email: [EMAIL]
Tel: [NUMBER]
Ref: [REFERENCE]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS
    ```
