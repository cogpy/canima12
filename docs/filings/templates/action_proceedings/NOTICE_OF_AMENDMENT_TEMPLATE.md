    # Notice of Amendment Template

    **Category:** Action Proceedings / Rule 28  
    **Purpose:** Notify other parties of an intended amendment to pleadings or documents.  
    **When not to use:** Do not assume the amendment is effective immediately if objection is delivered.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF INTENTION TO AMEND

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant / Applicant / Respondent] intends to amend [the pleading / notice / affidavit] in the manner set out in the schedule annexed hereto.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that unless written objection to the proposed amendment is delivered within 10 days after service hereof, the amendment will be effected accordingly.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
    ```
