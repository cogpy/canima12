## Template

```text
NOTICE OF EXCEPTION / APPLICATION TO STRIKE OUT

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant] hereby excepts to the
[particulars of claim / plea / replication / declaration] on the grounds that:

1. [It discloses no cause of action / no defence].
2. [It is vague and embarrassing].
3. [It contains scandalous, vexatious, or irrelevant matter], namely [detail].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the excipient will apply to this Honourable Court
for an order upholding the exception / striking out the impugned matter,
with costs, and for such further relief as may be just.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE OTHER PARTY / ATTORNEYS
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
```
