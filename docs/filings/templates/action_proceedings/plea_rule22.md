# Plea

    **Rule / source:** Rule 22  
**Use:** To answer particulars of claim in action proceedings.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use it as a substitute for an answering affidavit in application proceedings.

    ## Template

    ```text
    PLEA

Save where expressly admitted, the Defendant denies each allegation in the particulars of claim inconsistent herewith.

1. Ad paragraph 1 thereof: [admit / deny / no knowledge].
2. Ad paragraph 2 thereof: [response].
3. Ad paragraph 3 thereof: [response].

ALTERNATIVELY / FURTHER
4. The Defendant pleads that [special defence / prescription / jurisdiction / etc.].

WHEREFORE the Defendant prays that the Plaintiff's claim be dismissed with costs.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[DEFENDANT'S ATTORNEYS]
    ```
