# Notice of Amendment — Rule 28
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

## Template

```text
NOTICE OF INTENTION TO AMEND

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant / Applicant / Respondent]
intends to amend [the particulars of claim / plea / notice of motion /
affidavit] in the manner set out in the schedule annexed hereto.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that unless written objection to the proposed amendment
is delivered within 10 days after service hereof, the amendment will be
effected accordingly.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
```
