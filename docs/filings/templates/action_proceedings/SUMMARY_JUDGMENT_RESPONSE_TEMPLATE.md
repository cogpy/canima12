# Response to Summary Judgment Application

This template provides a defendant-side response wrapper where summary judgment is opposed by affidavit.

## Template

```text
AFFIDAVIT OPPOSING SUMMARY JUDGMENT

I, the undersigned,

[FULL NAME]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the Defendant / duly authorised representative of the Defendant.
2. I oppose the Plaintiff's application for summary judgment.
3. I bona fide defend the action and disclose fully the nature and grounds
   of my defence and the material facts relied upon therefor.
4. The defence is as follows:
   4.1 [defence 1];
   4.2 [defence 2];
   4.3 [defence 3].
5. The Plaintiff is accordingly not entitled to summary judgment.
6. I pray that the application be dismissed with costs and that I be granted
   leave to defend the action.

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
```
