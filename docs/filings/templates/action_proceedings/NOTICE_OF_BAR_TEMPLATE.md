    # Notice of Bar Template

    **Category:** Action Proceedings / Rule 26  
    **Purpose:** Call upon a defaulting pleader to deliver the next pleading within the rule period on pain of bar.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where no pleading obligation has yet arisen.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF BAR

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant] is hereby required, within 5 days after service hereof, to deliver [a plea / replication / further pleading], failing which [he / she / it] will be ipso facto barred.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE DEFAULTING PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
