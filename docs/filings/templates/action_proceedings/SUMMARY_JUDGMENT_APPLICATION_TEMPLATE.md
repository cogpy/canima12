# Summary Judgment Application — Rule 32
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

## Template

```text
NOTICE OF APPLICATION FOR SUMMARY JUDGMENT

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Plaintiff applies for summary judgment against
the Defendant for:

1. [Amount / delivery / ejectment / other liquidated relief].
2. Interest thereon at [rate].
3. Costs of suit.
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT], who can swear
positively to the facts and verify the cause of action and the amount
claimed, will be used in support of this application.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE DEFENDANT / ATTORNEYS
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
```
