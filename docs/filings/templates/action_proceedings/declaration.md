## Template

```text
DECLARATION

The Plaintiff hereby declares as follows:

1. The Plaintiff is [description].
2. The Defendant is [description].
3. On [date] the parties concluded [agreement / event] at [place].
4. The material terms were [terms].
5. The Defendant failed to perform in that [detail].
6. Despite demand on [date], the Defendant has failed / refused to remedy the breach.
7. The Plaintiff has suffered damages / is entitled to payment in the amount of [amount].

WHEREFORE the Plaintiff claims:
(a) [relief];
(b) interest; and
(c) costs.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS]
```
