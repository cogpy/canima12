**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

## Template

```text
SIMPLE SUMMONS

The Plaintiff claims from the Defendant:

1. Payment of the sum of [amount].
2. Interest thereon at the rate of [rate] from [date] to date of payment.
3. Costs of suit.
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

The cause of action is [briefly state liquidated claim].

YOU ARE HEREBY SUMMONED that within 10 court days after service of this
summons upon you, you give notice to the Plaintiff of your intention to
defend this action, failing which judgment may be granted against you.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS]
```
