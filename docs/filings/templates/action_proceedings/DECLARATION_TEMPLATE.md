    # Declaration Template

    **Category:** Action Proceedings / Rule 20  
    **Purpose:** Set out the plaintiff’s detailed cause of action after simple summons or where otherwise required.  
    **When not to use:** Do not confuse it with particulars of claim already included in a combined summons.

    ## Template

    ```text
    DECLARATION

The Plaintiff hereby declares as follows:

1. The Plaintiff is [description].
2. The Defendant is [description].
3. This Court has jurisdiction because [basis].
4. On [date] the parties concluded [agreement / event] at [place].
5. The material terms were [terms].
6. The Defendant failed to perform in that [detail].
7. Despite demand on [date], the Defendant has failed / refused to remedy the breach.
8. The Plaintiff has suffered damages / is entitled to payment in the amount of [amount].

WHEREFORE the Plaintiff claims:
(a) [relief];
(b) interest;
(c) costs; and
(d) further and/or alternative relief.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Plaintiff's Attorneys]
    ```
