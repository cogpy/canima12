```text
NOTICE OF APPLICATION FOR DEFAULT JUDGMENT

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Plaintiff applies for default judgment against
the Defendant on the following grounds:

1. The summons was duly served on [date].
2. The Defendant failed to deliver notice of intention to defend / plea
   within the prescribed time.
3. The Defendant is accordingly in default.
4. The Plaintiff is entitled to judgment for [relief].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] and the following
supporting documents will be used in support: [list].

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].
```
