# Rule 33 Stated Case or Point of Law Application

This template can be adapted either to a stated case agreed between the parties or to an application for the separate adjudication of a question of law.

## Template

```text
NOTICE OF APPLICATION IN TERMS OF RULE 33

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:

1. Directing that the following question(s) of law / issue(s) be decided
   separately before the hearing of the remaining issues:
   1.1 [question 1];
   1.2 [question 2].
2. Alternatively recording the stated case agreed between the parties as
   annexed hereto and directing that the same be adjudicated by the Court.
3. Staying the balance of the proceedings pending such adjudication, if necessary.
4. Costs in the cause, alternatively such order as the Court deems fit.
5. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT], if required, and the
annexed stated case / agreed facts will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / THEIR ATTORNEYS
```
