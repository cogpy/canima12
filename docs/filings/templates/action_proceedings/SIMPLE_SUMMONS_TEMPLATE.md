    # Simple Summons Template

    **Category:** Action Proceedings / Rule 17  
    **Purpose:** Commence an action for a liquidated claim or debt suitable for simple summons procedure.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where full particulars of claim are needed from the outset.

    ## Template

    ```text
    SIMPLE SUMMONS

The Plaintiff claims from the Defendant:

1. Payment of the sum of [amount].
2. Interest thereon at the rate of [rate] from [date] to date of payment.
3. Costs of suit.
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

The cause of action is [briefly state the liquidated claim].

YOU ARE HEREBY SUMMONED that within 10 court days after service of this summons upon you, you give notice to the Plaintiff of your intention to defend this action, failing which judgment may be granted against you.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Plaintiff's Attorneys]
    ```
