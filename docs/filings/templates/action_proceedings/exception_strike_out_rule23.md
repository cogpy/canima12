# Exception and/or Application to Strike Out

    **Rule / source:** Rule 23  
**Use:** To challenge a defective pleading or seek the striking out of scandalous, vexatious, or irrelevant matter.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use it to argue the merits where the complaint is not truly directed at the pleading itself.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF EXCEPTION / APPLICATION TO STRIKE OUT

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant] hereby excepts to the [particulars of claim / plea / replication / declaration] on the grounds that:

1. [It discloses no cause of action / no defence].
2. [It is vague and embarrassing].
3. [It contains scandalous, vexatious, or irrelevant matter], namely [detail].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the excipient will apply to this Honourable Court for an order upholding the exception / striking out the impugned matter, with costs, and for such further relief as may be just.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE OTHER PARTY / ATTORNEYS
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
    ```
