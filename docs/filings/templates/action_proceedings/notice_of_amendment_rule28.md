# Notice of Amendment

    **Rule / source:** Rule 28  
**Use:** To give notice of an intended amendment to a pleading or other document.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where leave of court is already required or where the amendment procedure has been superseded by court order.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF INTENTION TO AMEND

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant / Applicant / Respondent] intends to amend [the particulars of claim / plea / notice of motion / affidavit] in the manner set out in the schedule annexed hereto.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that unless written objection to the proposed amendment is delivered within 10 days after service hereof, the amendment will be effected accordingly.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
    ```
