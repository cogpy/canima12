# Notice of Bar

    **Rule / source:** Rule 26  
**Use:** To require delivery of an overdue pleading and place the defaulting party at risk of bar.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where no pleading is due or where the rules do not permit a bar in the specific context.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF BAR

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant] is hereby required, within 5 days after service hereof, to deliver [a plea / replication / other pleading], failing which [he/she/it] will be ipso facto barred.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE DEFAULTING PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
