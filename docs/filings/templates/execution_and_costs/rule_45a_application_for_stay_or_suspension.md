## Template

```text
NOTICE OF MOTION: APPLICATION IN TERMS OF RULE 45A

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:

1. Suspending / staying the operation and execution of the order dated [date]
   pending [appeal / rescission / review / further application].
2. Directing that no writ, attachment, or coercive step be executed pending
   final determination of such further proceedings.
3. Costs.
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL PARTIES
```
