    # Rule 47 Notice Requiring Security for Costs Template

    **Category:** Execution and Costs / Rule 47  
    **Purpose:** Require the opposing party to furnish security for costs before an application is launched to compel it.  
    **When not to use:** Do not skip the notice stage where the rule requires it.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 47

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [party requiring security] requires the [other party] to furnish security for costs in the sum of [amount], alternatively in such amount as may be determined by agreement or by the Court.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that such security is required within 10 days after service hereof, failing which application will be made for an order compelling the furnishing of security and staying the proceedings pending compliance.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE RECIPIENT PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
