# Rule 47 Security for Costs Notice

    **Rule / source:** Rule 47  
**Use:** To require an opposing party to furnish security for costs before application is brought.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use without a proper basis for security and an amount that can be justified.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 47

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Applicant / party requiring security] requires the [other party] to furnish security for costs in the sum of [R amount], alternatively in such amount as may be determined by agreement or by the Court.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that such security is required within 10 days after service hereof, failing which application will be made for an order compelling the furnishing of security and staying the proceedings pending compliance.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE RECIPIENT PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
