    # Rule 48 Review of Taxation Template

    **Category:** Execution and Costs / Rule 48  
    **Purpose:** Record a party’s challenge to taxation or allocatur and request the Rule 48 review process.  
    **When not to use:** Do not file without specifying the items and grounds of complaint with adequate precision.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF REVIEW OF TAXATION IN TERMS OF RULE 48

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [party dissatisfied with taxation] hereby gives notice of review of the taxation / allocatur of the Taxing Master dated [date], on the following grounds:

1. Item [number] was wrongly allowed / disallowed because [ground].
2. Item [number] was taxed at an unreasonable amount because [ground].
3. [Further grounds].

The party accordingly requests that the stated case / review be placed before the Court in accordance with Rule 48.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE TAXING MASTER
AND TO: ALL INTERESTED PARTIES
    ```
