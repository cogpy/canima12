# Rule 45A Stay / Suspension Application

    **Rule / source:** Rule 45A  
**Use:** To suspend or stay the operation and execution of an order pending further proceedings.  
    **When not to use:** Do not assume suspension is automatic where separate relief is required to preserve the status quo.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: APPLICATION IN TERMS OF RULE 45A

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:
1. Suspending / staying the operation and execution of the order dated [date] pending [appeal / rescission / review / further application].
2. Directing that no writ, attachment, or coercive step be executed pending final determination of such further proceedings.
3. Costs.
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL PARTIES
    ```
