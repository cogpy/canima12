# Rule 58 Interpleader Notice / Application

    **Rule / source:** Rule 58  
**Use:** To place competing claims to property or funds before the court where the stakeholder claims no beneficial interest.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where the stakeholder is itself asserting substantive ownership inconsistent with interpleader relief.

    ## Template

    ```text
    INTERPLEADER NOTICE / APPLICATION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant / Sheriff / stakeholder brings this interpleader in respect of [funds / property / goods] presently held by [stakeholder], to which competing claims are asserted by:

1. [Claimant 1]; and
2. [Claimant 2].

The Applicant / stakeholder:
(a) claims no beneficial interest in the said property / funds, save for charges and costs, if any;
(b) is unable safely to deliver the same without the direction of the Court; and
(c) accordingly seeks an order determining the rights of the claimants and authorising appropriate delivery / payment.

The Applicant / stakeholder further seeks costs out of the property / funds, alternatively as directed by the Court.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / SHERIFF / STAKEHOLDER]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL CLAIMANTS
    ```
