    # Rule 7 Notice Challenging Authority Template

    **Category:** Irregularity and Compliance / Rule 7  
    **Purpose:** Require proof that a person or firm purporting to act on behalf of a party is properly authorised.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use merely because you dispute the merits of the case.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 7(1)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Applicant / Respondent / Plaintiff / Defendant] hereby disputes the authority of [name / firm / representative], who has purported to act on behalf of [party], to so act in these proceedings.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the said [person / firm / representative] is required, within 10 court days of service hereof, to satisfy the Court that [he / she / it] is duly authorised to act on behalf of the said party.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that, failing such proof, the steps taken may be challenged and such further relief as may be appropriate may be sought.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys / Party in person]
TO: [Representative whose authority is challenged]
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / THEIR ATTORNEYS
    ```
