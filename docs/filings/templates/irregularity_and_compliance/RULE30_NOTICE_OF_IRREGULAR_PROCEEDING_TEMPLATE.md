    # Rule 30 Notice of Irregular Proceeding Template

    **Category:** Irregularity and Compliance / Rule 30  
    **Purpose:** Notify the opposing party that a step taken is irregular and call for removal of the cause of complaint.  
    **When not to use:** Do not conflate a Rule 30 irregular-step complaint with simple non-compliance more suited to Rule 30A.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 30

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Applicant / Respondent / Plaintiff / Defendant] contends that the [process / step] delivered on [date] constitutes an irregular proceeding / step on the following grounds:

1. [Ground 1].
2. [Ground 2].
3. [Ground 3].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the recipient is hereby called upon, within 10 court days after service hereof, to remove the cause of complaint, failing which application will be made to Court to set aside the said irregular step with costs.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE RECIPIENT PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
