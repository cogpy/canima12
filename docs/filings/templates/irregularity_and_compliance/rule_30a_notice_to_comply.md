## Template

```text
NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 30A

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Applicant / Respondent / Plaintiff / Defendant]
contends that you have failed to comply with [the Rules / the Court order
of [date] / the notice dated [date]] in the following respects:

1. [Non-compliance 1].
2. [Non-compliance 2].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that you are required, within 10 court days after
service hereof, to remedy the aforesaid non-compliance, failing which
application will be made for appropriate relief, including an order
compelling compliance, striking out the claim / defence, or dismissing the
proceedings, together with costs.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].
```
