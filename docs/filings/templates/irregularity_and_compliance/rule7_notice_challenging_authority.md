# Rule 7 Notice Challenging Authority to Act

    **Rule / source:** Rule 7(1)  
**Use:** To require proof of authority from a person purporting to act on behalf of a party.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use merely because you dispute the merits; the challenge must truly concern authority.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 7(1)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Applicant / Respondent / Plaintiff / Defendant] hereby disputes the authority of [NAME / FIRM / REPRESENTATIVE], who has purported to act on behalf of [PARTY], to so act in these proceedings.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the said [person / firm / representative] is required, within 10 court days of service hereof, to satisfy the Court that [he/she/it] is duly authorised to act on behalf of the said party.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that, failing such proof, the steps so taken may be challenged and such further relief as may be appropriate may be sought.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / PARTY IN PERSON]

TO: [PARTY / ATTORNEYS WHOSE AUTHORITY IS CHALLENGED]
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
    ```
