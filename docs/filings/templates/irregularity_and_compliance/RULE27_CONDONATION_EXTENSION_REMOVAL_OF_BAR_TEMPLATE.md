    # Rule 27 Condonation, Extension, or Removal of Bar Template

    **Category:** Irregularity and Compliance / Rule 27  
    **Purpose:** Seek an extension of time, removal of bar, and condonation for late delivery or filing.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use without a full explanation for delay and supporting affidavit material.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: RULE 27 APPLICATION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:

1. Extending the time within which [identified process] may be delivered to [date].
2. Removing the bar, if any, operating against the Applicant.
3. Condoning the late delivery / filing of [identified process].
4. Granting leave to deliver the annexed [plea / affidavit / notice / submission].
5. Costs [in the cause / as appropriate].
6. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [Deponent] will be used in support.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL PARTIES
    ```
