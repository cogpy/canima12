# Rule 30A Notice to Comply

    **Rule / source:** Rule 30A  
**Use:** To require compliance with the Rules, a court order, or a procedural obligation before seeking coercive relief.  
    **When not to use:** Do not confuse Rule 30A non-compliance with a true Rule 30 irregular-step complaint.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 30A

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Applicant / Respondent / Plaintiff / Defendant] contends that you have failed to comply with [the Rules / the Court order of [date] / the notice dated [date]] in the following respects:

1. [Non-compliance 1].
2. [Non-compliance 2].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that you are required, within 10 court days after service hereof, to remedy the aforesaid non-compliance, failing which application will be made for appropriate relief, including an order compelling compliance, striking out the claim / defence, or dismissing the proceedings, together with costs.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE DEFAULTING PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
