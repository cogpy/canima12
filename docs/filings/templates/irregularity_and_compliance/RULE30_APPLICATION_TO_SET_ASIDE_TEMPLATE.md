    # Rule 30 Application to Set Aside Irregular Proceeding Template

    **Category:** Irregularity and Compliance / Rule 30  
    **Purpose:** Apply to set aside an irregular step after the notice period has run without cure.  
    **When not to use:** Do not launch before giving the required prior notice unless clearly excused by rule or order.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: RULE 30 APPLICATION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant will apply for an order:

1. Declaring the [identified process / step] an irregular proceeding.
2. Setting aside the said process / step.
3. Directing the opposing party to pay the costs of this application.
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [Deponent] will be used in support.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES
    ```
