# Rule 30 Application to Set Aside Irregular Proceeding

    **Rule / source:** Rule 30  
**Use:** To seek a court order setting aside an irregular step after notice has been given and the complaint not cured.  
    **When not to use:** Do not skip the notice stage unless the procedural posture lawfully permits it.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: RULE 30 APPLICATION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant will apply for an order:
1. Declaring the [identified process / step] an irregular proceeding.
2. Setting aside the said process / step.
3. Directing the opposing party to pay the costs of this application.
4. Granting such further and/or alternative relief as the Court deems fit.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES
    ```
