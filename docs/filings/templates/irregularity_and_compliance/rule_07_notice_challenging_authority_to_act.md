# Rule 7 Notice Challenging Authority to Act — Rule 7
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

**Use:** To require proof of authority from a person purporting to act on behalf of a party.  
**When not to use:** Do not use against an admitted attorney of record merely because you dispute the merits.

## Template

```text
NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 7(1)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Applicant / Respondent / Defendant / Plaintiff]
hereby disputes the authority of [NAME / FIRM / REPRESENTATIVE], who has
purported to act on behalf of [PARTY], to so act in these proceedings.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the said [person / firm / representative] is
required, within 10 court days of service hereof, to satisfy the Court
that [he/she/it] is duly authorised to act on behalf of the said party.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that, failing such proof, the steps so taken may be
challenged and such further relief as may be appropriate may be sought.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEY / PARTY IN PERSON]

TO: [PARTY / ATTORNEYS WHOSE AUTHORITY IS CHALLENGED]
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
```
