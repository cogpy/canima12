# Rule 42 Rescission / Variation Application

    **Rule / source:** Rule 42  
**Use:** To seek rescission or variation of an order erroneously sought, erroneously granted, or otherwise falling within Rule 42.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where the complaint properly belongs to appeal alone or where the Rule 42 grounds are not identified.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: APPLICATION IN TERMS OF RULE 42

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:
1. Rescinding / varying the order granted on [date] under case number [number].
2. Declaring that the said order was erroneously sought and/or erroneously granted in the absence of the affected party, alternatively that it resulted from a mistake common to the parties, alternatively varying it to the extent set out below.
3. Costs of this application [as appropriate].
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL AFFECTED PARTIES
    ```
