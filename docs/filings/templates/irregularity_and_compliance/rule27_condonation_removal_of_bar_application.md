# Rule 27 Application for Extension of Time / Removal of Bar / Condonation

    **Rule / source:** Rule 27  
**Use:** To seek condonation, extend time periods, remove a bar, or obtain leave to file late process.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use without explaining the delay fully and candidly.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: RULE 27 APPLICATION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:
1. Extending the time within which [process] may be delivered to [date].
2. Removing the bar, if any, operating against the Applicant.
3. Condoning the late delivery / filing of [process].
4. Granting leave to deliver the annexed [process].
5. Costs [in the cause / as appropriate].
6. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL PARTIES
    ```
