# Rule 30A Application for Compliance / Relief

    **Rule / source:** Rule 30A  
**Use:** To compel compliance or seek consequential procedural relief after the non-compliance was not cured.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use it as a generic substitute for case-management frustration or merits disagreement.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: RULE 30A APPLICATION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:
1. Directing the Respondent to comply with [the Rule / order / notice] within [period];
2. Alternatively striking out the Respondent's [claim / defence / affidavit / pleading];
3. Alternatively dismissing the proceedings / defence, as the case may be;
4. Costs of this application; and
5. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL PARTIES
    ```
