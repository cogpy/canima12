    # Rule 30A Application Template

    **Category:** Irregularity and Compliance / Rule 30A  
    **Purpose:** Seek an order compelling compliance or striking out non-compliant process after a Rule 30A notice has not been cured.  
    **When not to use:** Do not omit the prior notice stage unless a clear procedural basis exists.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: RULE 30A APPLICATION

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:

1. Directing the Respondent to comply with [the Rule / order / notice] within [period].
2. Alternatively striking out the Respondent's [claim / defence / affidavit / pleading].
3. Alternatively dismissing / staying the affected proceedings, as the case may be.
4. Costs of this application.
5. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [Deponent] will be used in support.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL PARTIES
    ```
