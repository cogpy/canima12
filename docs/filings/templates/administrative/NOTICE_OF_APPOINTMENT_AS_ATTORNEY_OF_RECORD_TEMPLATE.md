    # Notice of Appointment as Attorney of Record Template

    **Category:** Administrative / Representation  
    **Purpose:** Record a new attorney of record or substitution of attorneys in a matter.  
    **When not to use:** Do not assume this notice alone grants CourtOnline access; file the platform access request separately where needed.

    ## Template

    ```text
    IN THE HIGH COURT OF SOUTH AFRICA
([DIVISION])

CASE NO: [YYYY-NNNNNN]

In the matter between:
[PARTY NAMES AS CITED]

NOTICE OF APPOINTMENT AS ATTORNEY OF RECORD

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT [Firm Name] Attorneys have been appointed as attorneys of record for the [Applicant / Respondent / Plaintiff / Defendant] in the above matter, in place of [former attorneys / party acting in person].

All further process, notices and documents shall be served at the address set out below.

DATED at [Place] on this [Day] day of [Month] [Year].

______________________________
[Firm Name]
Attorneys for the [Party]
Address for service: [Address]
Email: [Email]
Tel: [Number]
Ref: [Reference]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / THEIR ATTORNEYS
    ```
