# Notice of Intention to Oppose

This template captures the standard motion-proceedings gateway notice that must not be confused with a notice of intention to defend in action proceedings.

## Template

```text
NOTICE OF INTENTION TO OPPOSE

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [First / Second / n-th Respondent] hereby gives
notice of [his/her/its] intention to oppose the application instituted by
the Applicant under the above case number.

The Respondent appoints the address set out below as the address at which
all further process and notices in this application may be served.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / RESPONDENT IN PERSON]
Address for service: [ADDRESS]
Email: [EMAIL]
Tel: [NUMBER]
Ref: [REFERENCE]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: THE APPLICANT'S ATTORNEYS
```
