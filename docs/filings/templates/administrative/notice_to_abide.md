# Notice to Abide

This template is for a party who elects not to oppose the merits actively but wishes to remain bound to the procedural outcome and preserve notice rights.

## Template

```text
NOTICE TO ABIDE

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [party description] abides the decision of the
above Honourable Court in the application / action under the above case
number.

The said party does not, by this notice alone, consent to any adverse costs
order beyond such order as the Court may deem appropriate, and reserves all
rights in relation to costs unless expressly stated otherwise herein.

The address below is appointed as the address at which all further notices,
if any, may be served.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS / PARTY IN PERSON]
Address for service: [ADDRESS]
Email: [EMAIL]
Tel: [NUMBER]
Ref: [REFERENCE]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / THEIR ATTORNEYS
```
