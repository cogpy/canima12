    # Notice to Abide Template

    **Category:** Administrative / Party Positioning  
    **Purpose:** Allow a nominal or neutral party to remain in the proceedings without actively opposing the substantive relief.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where the party in truth disputes the merits or seeks affirmative relief.

    ## Template

    ```text
    IN THE HIGH COURT OF SOUTH AFRICA
([DIVISION])

CASE NO: [YYYY-NNNNNN]

In the matter between:
[PARTY NAMES AS CITED]

NOTICE TO ABIDE

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Party] hereby abides the decision of this Honourable Court in respect of the relief sought in the present proceedings.

The said party reserves its / his / her rights only in relation to costs [if applicable: and will seek an order that costs be in the cause / no order as to costs].

DATED at [Place] on this [Day] day of [Month] [Year].

______________________________
[Attorney / Party in person]
Address for service: [Address]
Email: [Email]
Tel: [Number]

TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / THEIR ATTORNEYS
    ```
