    # Rule 36 Expert Notice and Summary Template

    **Category:** Discovery and Trial / Rule 36  
    **Purpose:** Give notice of intention to call an expert witness and furnish a summary of the expert’s opinion.  
    **When not to use:** Do not provide a bare notice without the required expert summary where the rule and directives require it.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 36(9)(a) AND (b)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant] intends to call [Expert Name], a [profession / qualification], as an expert witness at the trial.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the summary of the expert's opinion and reasons therefor is annexed hereto marked "A".

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
    ```
