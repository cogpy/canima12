# Expert Notice and Summary

    **Rule / source:** Rule 36(9)(a) and (b)  
**Use:** To notify the intention to call an expert and provide the required summary of opinion.  
    **When not to use:** Do not call expert evidence without complying with the applicable notice and summary requirements.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 36(9)(a) AND (b)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Defendant] intends to call [EXPERT NAME], a [profession / qualification], as an expert witness at the trial.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the summary of the expert's opinion and reasons therefor is annexed hereto marked "A".

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
    ```
