    # Discovery Affidavit Template

    **Category:** Discovery and Trial / Rule 35  
    **Purpose:** Disclose documents and recordings in possession or under control relating to matters in question.  
    **When not to use:** Do not omit privileged documents from a separate privileged schedule where the Rules require disclosure of existence.

    ## Template

    ```text
    DISCOVERY AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[Full Name]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the [party / authorised representative of the party] in this action.
2. I have in my possession or under my control the documents and recordings relating to the matters in question listed in the schedules annexed hereto.
3. Save as appears from the said schedules, I do not have in my possession or under my control any other documents or recordings relating to the matters in question.

FIRST SCHEDULE
[Documents available for inspection]

SECOND SCHEDULE
[Privileged documents briefly described]

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
    ```
