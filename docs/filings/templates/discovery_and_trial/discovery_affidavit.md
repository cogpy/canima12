# Discovery Affidavit — Rule 35(1)
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

## Template

```text
DISCOVERY AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[FULL NAME]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the [party / authorised representative of the party] in this action.
2. I have in my possession / under my control the documents and tape
   recordings relating to the matters in question listed in the First and
   Second Schedules hereto.
3. Save as appears from the said schedules, I do not have in my possession
   or under my control any other documents or recordings relating to the
   matters in question.

FIRST SCHEDULE
[Documents available for inspection]

SECOND SCHEDULE
[Privileged documents, briefly described without disclosing privileged content]

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
```
