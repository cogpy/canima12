# Summary Judgment Application

    **Rule / source:** Rule 32  
**Use:** To seek summary judgment where the claim falls within the rule and no bona fide defence is disclosed.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use where the claim or defence falls outside the rule or the procedure is otherwise unavailable.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF APPLICATION FOR SUMMARY JUDGMENT

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Plaintiff applies for summary judgment against the Defendant for:
1. [Amount / delivery / ejectment / other liquidated relief].
2. Interest thereon at [rate].
3. Costs of suit.
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT], who can swear positively to the facts and verify the cause of action and the amount claimed, will be used in support of this application.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE DEFENDANT / ATTORNEYS
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
    ```
