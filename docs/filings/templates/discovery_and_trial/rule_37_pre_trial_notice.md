# Rule 37 Pre-Trial Notice
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

## Template

```text
NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 37(2)(b) AND (7)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Plaintiff calls upon the Defendant(s) to hold a
pre-trial conference on:

Date: [DATE]
Time: [TIME]
Venue: [VENUE / VIRTUAL PLATFORM]

The following matters will be canvassed:
1. Settlement prospects.
2. Admissions.
3. Issues in dispute and not in dispute.
4. Documents and real evidence.
5. Witnesses and expert evidence.
6. Separation of issues and quantum.
7. Trial duration and availability.
8. Costs and other ancillary matters.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS]
TO: DEFENDANT'S ATTORNEYS
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
```
