# Rule 35(3) Notice for Further Discovery

    **Rule / source:** Rule 35(3)  
**Use:** To require additional relevant documents believed to exist in the possession or control of the other party.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use it speculatively without identifying the requested categories with adequate precision.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 35(3)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [requesting party] requires the [recipient party] to make available for inspection / produce the following documents or tape recordings, which are believed to be in your possession or under your control and relevant to the matters in question:

1. [Document category].
2. [Document category].
3. [Document category].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the said documents are required within 10 days of service hereof, failing which application will be made for appropriate relief.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE RECIPIENT PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
