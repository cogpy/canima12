# Discovery Affidavit

    **Rule / source:** Rule 35(1)  
**Use:** To disclose documents and recordings relating to the matters in question.  
    **When not to use:** Do not omit privileged materials from the privileged schedule or discoverable materials from the main schedule.

    ## Template

    ```text
    DISCOVERY AFFIDAVIT

I, the undersigned,

[FULL NAME]

do hereby make oath and say:

1. I am the [party / authorised representative of the party] in this action.
2. I have in my possession / under my control the documents and tape recordings relating to the matters in question listed in the First and Second Schedules hereto.
3. Save as appears from the said schedules, I do not have in my possession or under my control any other documents or recordings relating to the matters in question.

FIRST SCHEDULE
[Documents available for inspection]

SECOND SCHEDULE
[Privileged documents, briefly described without disclosing privileged content]

DEPONENT: __________________
COMMISSIONER OF OATHS: __________________
    ```
