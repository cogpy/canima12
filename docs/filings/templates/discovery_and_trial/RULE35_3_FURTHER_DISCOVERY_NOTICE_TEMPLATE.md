    # Rule 35(3) Further Discovery Notice Template

    **Category:** Discovery and Trial / Rule 35  
    **Purpose:** Require production or discovery of further documents believed to be relevant and under the control of another party.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use as a fishing expedition without identifying the documents or categories with reasonable precision.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE IN TERMS OF RULE 35(3)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [requesting party] requires the [recipient party] to make available for inspection / produce the following documents or recordings, which are believed to be in your possession or under your control and relevant to the matters in question:

1. [Document category].
2. [Document category].
3. [Document category].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the said documents are required within 10 days of service hereof, failing which application will be made for appropriate relief.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE RECIPIENT PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
