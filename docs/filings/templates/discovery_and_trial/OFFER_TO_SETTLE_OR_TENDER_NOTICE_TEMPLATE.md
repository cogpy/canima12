# Offer to Settle / Tender Notice

    **Rule / source:** Rule 34  
**Use:** To make a procedural offer or tender with cost consequences under the rule.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use without considering the precise Rule 34 subprocedure and cost implications applicable to the case.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF OFFER TO SETTLE / TENDER

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT, without prejudice save as to costs and in terms of Rule 34, the [Plaintiff / Defendant / Applicant / Respondent] hereby offers / tenders as follows:

1. [Payment / performance / undertaking].
2. [Interest / timing / method].
3. [Costs proposal].

This offer / tender remains open for acceptance until [date / event], failing which it shall lapse unless withdrawn earlier in accordance with the Rules.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE OTHER PARTY / ATTORNEYS
    ```
