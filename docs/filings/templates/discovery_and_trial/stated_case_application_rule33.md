# Stated Case / Point of Law Application

    **Rule / source:** Rule 33  
**Use:** To obtain adjudication on a stated case or a separated question of law where appropriate.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use as a substitute for full trial preparation where the facts are not sufficiently agreed or separable.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF APPLICATION IN TERMS OF RULE 33

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order:
1. Directing that the following question(s) of law or stated case be adjudicated separately: [identify issue].
2. Directing the parties to sign and file the agreed statement of facts annexed hereto / to be finalized in accordance with the Court's directions.
3. Costs [in the cause / as appropriate].
4. Further and/or alternative relief.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT], if required, and the annexed draft stated case / agreed facts will be used in support.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL PARTIES
    ```
