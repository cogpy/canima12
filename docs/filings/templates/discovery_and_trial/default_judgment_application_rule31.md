# Default Judgment Application

    **Rule / source:** Rule 31  
**Use:** To seek default judgment after failure to defend or plead as the Rules permit.  
    **When not to use:** Do not use unless the relevant default has actually occurred and service is regular.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF APPLICATION FOR DEFAULT JUDGMENT

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Plaintiff applies for default judgment against the Defendant on the following grounds:
1. The summons was duly served on [date].
2. The Defendant failed to deliver notice of intention to defend / plea within the prescribed time.
3. The Defendant is accordingly in default.
4. The Plaintiff is entitled to judgment for [relief].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [DEPONENT] and the following supporting documents will be used in support: [list].

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[PLAINTIFF'S ATTORNEYS]
    ```
