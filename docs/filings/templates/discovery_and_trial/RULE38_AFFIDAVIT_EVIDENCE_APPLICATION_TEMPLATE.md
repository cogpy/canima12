    # Rule 38 Affidavit-Evidence Application Template

    **Category:** Discovery and Trial / Rule 38  
    **Purpose:** Apply to adduce identified evidence at trial by affidavit where permitted.  
    **When not to use:** Do not assume affidavit evidence is automatically admissible at trial without leave where required.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF MOTION: APPLICATION IN TERMS OF RULE 38(2)

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the Applicant applies for an order authorising the following evidence at trial to be adduced by affidavit:

1. [Witness name].
2. [Subject of evidence].

The grounds for the application are:
(a) [reason 1];
(b) [reason 2]; and
(c) such course will not prejudice the opposing party, alternatively any prejudice can be cured by directions of the Court.

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the affidavit of [Deponent] will be used in support.

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: THE REGISTRAR
AND TO: ALL PARTIES
    ```
