    # Notice of Withdrawal or Discontinuance Template

    **Category:** Settlement and Mediation / Rule 41  
    **Purpose:** Withdraw or discontinue an action, application, or claim, usually with a costs position stated.  
    **When not to use:** Do not omit the costs tender or costs position where the rule or practice requires clarity.

    ## Template

    ```text
    NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL / DISCONTINUANCE

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Applicant] hereby withdraws / discontinues the [action / application / claim] against the [Defendant / Respondent], subject to [if any condition on costs or settlement terms].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the [withdrawing party] tenders / does not tender costs [as set out below].

DATED at [Place] on [Date].

______________________________
[Attorneys]
TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
    ```
