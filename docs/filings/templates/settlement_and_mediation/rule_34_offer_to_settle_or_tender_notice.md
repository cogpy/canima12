# Rule 34 Offer to Settle or Tender Notice

This template provides a settlement / tender notice aimed at managing exposure, narrowing issues, and shaping costs consequences.

## Template

```text
NOTICE OF OFFER TO SETTLE / TENDER IN TERMS OF RULE 34

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT, without admission of liability save to the extent
expressly set out below, the [Defendant / party making the offer] hereby
offers / tenders the following in full and final settlement of the
Plaintiff's claim / specified issues:

1. Payment of the amount of [AMOUNT], alternatively [other performance].
2. Interest thereon at [RATE], if applicable.
3. Costs [up to a specified date / on a specified scale], if applicable.
4. Such offer remains open for acceptance until [DATE / TIME].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that if the offer is not accepted and the eventual
result is no more favourable to the opposing party, reliance may be placed
on this notice in relation to costs.

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: THE OPPOSING PARTY / ATTORNEYS
```
