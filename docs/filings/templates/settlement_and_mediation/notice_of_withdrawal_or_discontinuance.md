# Notice of Withdrawal / Discontinuance — Rule 41
This template was materialized from the consolidated High Court precedent library so that it can be used directly from the dedicated templates folder.
**Source:** [Uniform Rules Template Library (2026-04-22)](../reference/UNIFORM_RULES_TEMPLATE_LIBRARY_2026-04-22.md)

## Template

```text
NOTICE OF WITHDRAWAL / DISCONTINUANCE

KINDLY TAKE NOTICE THAT the [Plaintiff / Applicant] hereby withdraws /
discontinues the [action / application / claim] against the [Defendant /
Respondent], subject to [if any condition on costs or settlement terms].

TAKE NOTICE FURTHER that the [withdrawing party] tenders / does not tender
costs [as set out below].

DATED at [PLACE] on this [DAY] day of [MONTH] [YEAR].

__________________________________
[ATTORNEYS]
TO: ALL OTHER PARTIES / ATTORNEYS
AND TO: THE REGISTRAR
```
