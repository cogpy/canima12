## Filing-Ready Core Submission

> On 4 May 2026, Labuschagne J dismissed the applicant's application for leave to appeal. The court held that clause 2.5 of the 19 August 2025 order merely prohibits prejudicial conduct and does not preclude Mrs Faucitt from being involved in the businesses in any manner. The court further held that the applicant did not establish breach by mere say-so, that the respondent's version was properly accepted on Plascon-Evans principles, and that there were neither reasonable prospects of another court reaching a different conclusion nor any other compelling reason for the appeal to be heard.
