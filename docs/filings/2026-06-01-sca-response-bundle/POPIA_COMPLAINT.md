## Complaint statement

The complainants request that the Information Regulator investigate the processing of personal information described in this complaint. The Regulator’s complaint page states that general POPIA complaints may be lodged where a person believes personal information has been processed in a way that violates POPIA, including unauthorised sharing, collection without knowledge or consent, unlawful processing of sensitive or special personal information, or failure to respond to POPIA rights requests.[19]

## Prior steps and urgency

Before filing, the complainants should add a schedule of any prior notices, objections, PAIA/POPIA requests, information-officer correspondence or reasons why direct prior engagement is unsafe, futile or urgent. If a prior request has not been made, the filing copy should explain whether the complaint is lodged because court-record use and continuing disclosure create ongoing prejudice requiring regulatory intervention.

## Remedial action requested

The complainants request that the Regulator register the complaint; require the responsible parties to identify information officers and operators; require preservation of processing records, access logs and disclosure logs; investigate whether personal and special personal information was processed lawfully, minimally, accurately and securely; require corrective notices, deletion, restriction, correction or undertakings where appropriate; and consider enforcement or referral steps if Chapter 11 thresholds are implicated.[17] [19]
