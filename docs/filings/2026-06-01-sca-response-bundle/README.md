## Bundle rationale

The 1 June 2026 petition is not merely another annexure. It is a live appellate step seeking to revive the dismissed contempt application and it continues to invoke the 19 August 2025 order as an operative control instrument. The response strategy must therefore be dual-track. The first track is an immediate and technical SCA opposition defending Labuschagne J’s contempt dismissal under section 17 of the Superior Courts Act and the *Fakie* contempt framework. The second track is a structural Rule 42/common-law rescission response that attacks the foundation order on fraud, perjury, material non-disclosure, and legal-impossibility grounds.[1] [2] [4] [5] [6]
