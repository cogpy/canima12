## Purpose of this bundle

This folder contains the **v33 consolidated complete filing set**. The objective is to keep the GitHub Pages evidence reference bundle organized around seven forum-specific filings while preserving a common evidence spine. The civil track addresses the foundation order, Rule 42/common-law rescission and opposition to the 1 June 2026 SCA petition. The investigative and regulatory tracks route distinct issues to SAPS/Commercial Crime, the NPA, SARS, CIPC and the Information Regulator using threshold-safe language that requests investigation rather than asserting final criminal guilt.[1] [2] [3]

The v33 consolidation keeps the strongest features of all prior versions. It uses the current 2026-06-01 bundle as the structural base because it already operates as a complete submission set. It then incorporates the fuller notice-of-motion relief, void-ab-initio analysis, perjury-with-provable-knowledge logic, commercial-crime taxonomy, tax-risk categories, Companies Act heads and POPIA incident framing from earlier repository versions.[4] [5] [6] [7] [8] [9]

## Filing order recommendation

The recommended immediate order remains: **SCA opposition first**, **Rule 42 rescission in parallel**, then **criminal and regulatory referrals after final evidence pagination**. This sequence avoids allowing the SCA petition to proceed undefended while still attacking the root order. It also preserves the correct burden separation: civil issues proceed on rescission, procedural-integrity and balance-of-probabilities reasoning; criminal issues remain framed as investigation requests until a prosecutor assesses proof beyond reasonable doubt.[2] [3] [10]
