> **Verification note.** This v34 pack corrects the format issue identified after v33. The evidence synthesis remains consolidated, but each document now opens in the procedural style expected by its receiving forum. Court filings are structured as notices, draft orders and affidavits; criminal material is structured as a SAPS complainant statement; NPA and SARS materials are referral letters; the CIPC document is a CoR135.1-style complaint narrative; and the POPIA document is an Information Regulator complaint statement. The filing party must still verify prescribed forms, service addresses, commissioning, annexure numbering, page references and redactions before submission.

## Purpose of this bundle

This folder contains the **v34 body-specific filing pack**. The objective is to keep the GitHub Pages evidence reference bundle organized around seven forum-specific submissions while avoiding the earlier defect of making every submission resemble a general legal memorandum. The civil track addresses the foundation order, Rule 42/common-law rescission and opposition to the 1 June 2026 SCA petition. The investigative and regulatory tracks route distinct issues to SAPS/Commercial Crime, the NPA, SARS, CIPC and the Information Regulator in the form most likely to be understood by each intake body.[1] [2] [3]

The v34 revision does not discard the v33 evidence work. It preserves the consolidated evidence spine, historical source map and annexure schedule, but changes the front-end filing posture: **affidavit where an affidavit is required, complainant statement where SAPS is the recipient, prescribed-form annexure where CIPC is the recipient, and referral letter where prosecutorial or tax screening is requested**.[4] [5] [6] [7] [8] [9]

## Forum-specific filing order recommendation

The recommended immediate sequence remains **SCA opposition first**, **Rule 42 rescission in parallel**, then **SAPS / NPA / SARS / CIPC / POPIA filings after final evidence pagination**. The reason is procedural: the SCA petition must not remain unanswered, while the Rule 42 application attacks the root order and the investigative/regulatory filings preserve separate burden standards. Civil documents may proceed on rescission, procedural-integrity and balance-of-probabilities reasoning; criminal documents remain framed as requests for investigation until prosecutors assess proof beyond reasonable doubt.[2] [3] [10]
