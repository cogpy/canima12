## Executive Summary

The strongest remaining defence strategy is not factual contradiction but **presentation attack**. The respondent-side filing set is already rich in evidence. The risk lies in allowing the defence to manufacture doubt from chronology compression, entity slippage, authorized-access reframing, and quantum overreach. The refinements completed on 2026-04-21 materially reduce those risks.

This addendum should therefore be used as a **rebuttal calibration layer**. It tells each filing what must now be said expressly to defeat the most credible remaining attacks.
