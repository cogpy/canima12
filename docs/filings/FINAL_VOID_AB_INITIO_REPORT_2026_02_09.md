The invalidity of the order rests on a combination of seven distinct pillars and patterns of fraud:

1.  **Legal Impossibility:** The interdict was based on a claim of "unauthorized transactions." This was legally impossible, as the FNB mandates, signed by Peter Faucitt himself, granted all directors SOLE authority.
2.  **Perjury with Foreknowledge:** On 18 June 2025, FNB Legal explicitly informed Peter Faucitt via email that all directors could act independently. Two months later, he swore the opposite under oath.
3.  **Material Non-Disclosure:** Peter Faucitt concealed the SOLE mandate and the FNB Legal letter from the court.
4.  **Supporting Affidavit Fraud:** The supporting affiant, Danie Bantjies, had received a comprehensive fraud report from Daniel Faucitt on 6 June 2025, yet signed a false affidavit on 1 August 2025.
5.  **Direct Admission with Concealment:** In his own affidavit, Peter Faucitt admits to cancelling Daniel Faucitt's business cards but conceals that he did so secretly and without authority.
6.  **Fabrication of Evidence:** Rynette Farrar instructed accountants to create fake 2019 financial statements for a company that did not exist under that name until 2021. Peter Faucitt signed these as director.
7.  **Weaponized Litigation:** The perpetrators have repeatedly used the courts as an instrument of fraud, suing their victims for "harassment" for attempting to expose the crimes, and using settlement agreements to impose psychiatric evaluations to discredit them as witnesses.
