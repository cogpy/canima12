# EVENT_108: Board Resolution to Remove Director P.A. Faucitt

**Date:** 2026-02-16

## Description

A formal board resolution package was prepared for the removal of Peter Andrew Faucitt as director of Regima Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd under Section 71(3) of the Companies Act.

## Significance

This action, based on neglect and dereliction of duty, represents a formal move by the majority of the board to legally remove the primary perpetrator from his position of power within the company. It is a direct consequence of the evidence of fraud and operational sabotage.
