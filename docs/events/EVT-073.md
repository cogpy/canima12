## Description

On 11 September 2025, a payment of approximately **ZAR 90,000** was made from the RegimA Skin Treatments CC bank account (55270035642) to Elliott Attorneys. This payment was made on the same date that consolidated FNB account statements show approximately ZAR 10 million was emptied from ALL company current accounts.

**June 2026 update:** this event should now be read together with [EVENT_181](./EVENT_181.md), which records a broader ABSA payment schedule showing **R478,957.93** paid by RegimA Skin Treatments CC to Elliott Attorneys between **25 August 2025** and **15 April 2026**.

## Forensic Significance

This payment, combined with EVT-072 (VVA paying ZAR 300k to ENS), establishes that entity funds were routinely used for legal costs. The later position taken by the applicant — that entity funds should not cover the respondents' legal costs — is directly contradicted by these payments. Furthermore, the payment to Elliott Attorneys on the same day that ZAR 10 million was removed from company accounts suggests coordinated action. [EVENT_181](./EVENT_181.md) expands this issue into a sustained RST-funded legal-cost anomaly requiring disclosure of the mandate, invoices, corporate authority, and conflict checks.
