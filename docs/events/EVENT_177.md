## Description
Elliott Attorneys, acting for Peter Andrew Faucitt under reference **KRE/KF0019**, filed a **Notice of Application for Leave to Appeal** in Case 2025/137857. The notice seeks leave to appeal to the Full Bench of the Gauteng Division, Pretoria, alternatively the Supreme Court of Appeal, against the whole judgment and order delivered by Justice Labuschagne on 26 March 2026.

The notice frames the appeal as having both a **reasonable prospect of success** and **compelling reasons** under section 17 of the Superior Courts Act 10 of 2013.
